DMM Test Steps
  1. De-energize and verify no voltage.

Troubleshooting
  • Low ohms: inspect nearby boxes and terminations.
  • Ground fault: check staples, conduit edges, wet boxes, shields/drain wires.
  • High ohms: look for corrosion, bad splices, device still connected, partial opens.
